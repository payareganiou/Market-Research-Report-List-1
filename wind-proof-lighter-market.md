<p><p>The wind-proof lighter market is primarily categorized into gas lighters and liquid lighters. Gas lighters utilize pressurized gas, typically butane, which ignites quickly and produces a strong flame resistant to wind, making them ideal for outdoor use. Liquid lighters, on the other hand, rely on lighter fluid, offering a longer-lasting burn and are often refillable. Both types are popular for their durability and effectiveness in adverse weather conditions, catering to diverse consumer needs for reliability and convenience.</p></p>
