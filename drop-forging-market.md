<p><strong>The Drop Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Die Drop Forging</li><li>Closed Die Drop Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drop forging market is primarily categorized into two types: open die and closed die forging. Open die drop forging involves shaping metal between two flat dies, allowing for larger and simpler shapes, while accommodating various materials and sizes. Closed die drop forging, on the other hand, uses shaped dies to create more complex and precise parts with better dimensional accuracy. Both methods cater to different industrial needs, with open die being ideal for larger pieces and closed die for intricate components.</p></p>
