<p><p>Rust Buster products are categorized into three market types based on their pH levels: Acidity, Alkaline, and Neutral. Acidic Rust Busters, typically featuring a low pH, effectively dissolve rust and corrosion but may require careful handling. Alkaline Rust Busters with a high pH are excellent for tackling tougher rust while being less corrosive to certain materials. Neutral Rust Busters, with a balanced pH, offer a milder approach, suitable for delicate surfaces and regular maintenance without harsh effects.</p></p>
