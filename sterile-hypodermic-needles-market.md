<p><p>The market for sterile hypodermic needles is categorized primarily into short bevel and long bevel needles. Short bevel needles feature a bevel that is less pronounced, making them ideal for quick injections and minimizing tissue damage, commonly used in intravenous procedures. Conversely, long bevel needles possess a more acute angle, which facilitates smoother penetration, making them preferable for subcutaneous and intramuscular injections. The choice between these types depends on the application, patient comfort, and the required precision of drug delivery.</p></p>
