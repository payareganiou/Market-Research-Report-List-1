<p><strong>The Mechanical Pencils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-based</li><li>Ratchet-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical pencil market consists of two primary types: screw-based and ratchet-based mechanisms. Screw-based pencils utilize a twisting action to advance the lead, offering precise control over lead length and reducing lead breakage. In contrast, ratchet-based mechanical pencils use a push-click mechanism, allowing users to extend the lead in small increments with each click. While screw-based models are often favored for their durability and precision, ratchet-based options provide convenience and ease of use, appealing to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
