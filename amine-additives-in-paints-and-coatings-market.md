<p><p>Amine additives in paints and coatings enhance performance and application properties. AMP (Amino Methyl Propanol) improves adhesion and reduces surface defects, while DMAMP (Dimethyl Amino Methyl Propanol) aids in pH adjustment and stability. DMAE (Dimethyl Amino Ethanol) enhances gloss and drying properties, and DEAE (Diethyl Amino Ethanol) serves as a co-solvent, improving solubility and compatibility with various resins. Additional amine types contribute specific functionalities tailored to diverse coating formulations, optimizing durability and application characteristics.</p></p>
