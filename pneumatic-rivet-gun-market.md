<p><p>The pneumatic rivet gun market features several specialized types. Core pulling rivets are designed for applications requiring the extraction of a core or mandrel during riveting, providing strong joints in various materials. Rivet nut guns facilitate the installation of threaded inserts into thin materials, allowing for secure fastening. Ring groove rivet guns are utilized for creating riveted joints in applications with circular grooves, ensuring consistent and reliable performance in demanding environments. Each type caters to specific industry needs, enhancing assembly efficiency.</p></p>
