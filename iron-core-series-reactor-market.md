<p><p>The iron core series reactor market is primarily categorized into dry type and oil-immersed type reactors. Dry type reactors, which use air as the cooling medium, are known for their compact design and low maintenance, making them suitable for installations in urban settings. In contrast, oil-immersed reactors utilize oil for cooling, providing better heat dissipation and higher efficiency, often preferred in high-voltage applications. Both types cater to different operational requirements and are essential in stabilizing electrical systems and managing harmonics.</p></p>
