<p><p>The Pre-Hospital Blood Warmer market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive blood heaters. Invasive pre-hospital blood heaters involve direct contact with blood, typically used in emergency situations to quickly warm transfusions during trauma or surgery. Non-invasive pre-hospital blood heaters provide an external method for warming blood without direct contact, often employing heated blankets or devices that warm the surrounding environment. Both types aim to prevent hypothermia and ensure optimal blood temperature in critical care settings before reaching a medical facility.</p></p>
