<p><p>The semi-insulating SiC wafer market primarily consists of two types: 4H-SiC and 6H-SiC. 4H-SiC wafers, characterized by their hexagonal crystal structure, offer superior electronic properties, making them suitable for high-power and high-frequency applications. Conversely, 6H-SiC wafers have a slightly different crystal arrangement, providing good thermal conductivity and electrical performance. Both types are used in semiconductor devices, such as power electronics and RF components, serving diverse industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and renewable energy.</p></p>
