<p><strong>The Polyester Artificial Marble Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pour Forming</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Bulk Scrap Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester artificial marble market consists of three primary types: Pour Forming, Compression Molding, and Bulk Scrap Molding. Pour Forming involves pouring a resin mixture into molds, allowing for intricate designs and high-quality finishes. Compression Molding uses a heated mold to compress the material, providing dense and durable products. Bulk Scrap Molding recycles leftover polyester materials into new products, promoting sustainability and cost-effectiveness. Each method caters to specific applications, showcasing versatility and innovation within the artificial marble industry.</p></p>
