<p><p>Neoprene elastic gaskets are vital for sealing applications across various industries, with types including saddle and waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets feature a rounded shape that enhances surface contact, providing effective sealing in joint applications. Waveform gaskets, characterized by their undulating design, allow for flexibility and adaptability to uneven surfaces, ensuring a tight seal even under varying pressures. Both types are essential in preventing leaks and ensuring durability in environments exposed to moisture, chemicals, and temperature fluctuations.</p></p>
