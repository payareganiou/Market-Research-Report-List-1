<p><strong>The Gastrointestinal Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Covered Gastrointestinal Stents</li><li>Partially Covered Gastrointestinal Stents</li><li>Non-Covered Gastrointestinal Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gastrointestinal stent market comprises three main types: fully covered, partially covered, and non-covered stents. Fully covered stents are completely enveloped in a polymer, preventing tissue ingrowth, while partially covered stents feature both covered and uncovered sections, allowing for some tissue adherence. Non-covered stents, made of bare metal mesh, facilitate maximum lumen patency but risk tissue in-growth. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, with variations in their application based on factors like treatment goals and patient conditions.</p></p>
