<p><p>The illumination of the microscope market is primarily categorized into LED and fluorescence types. LED illumination offers energy efficiency, long lifespan, and low heat emission, making it popular for routine observations. Fluorescence illumination enables enhanced visibility of specific cellular components through selective excitation, allowing for detailed analysis in research and medical applications. Both types cater to diverse microscopy needs, with LED systems favored for general use and fluorescence setups preferred for specialized, high-contrast imaging in biological and material sciences.</p></p>
