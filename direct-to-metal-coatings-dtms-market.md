<p><p>Direct-to-Metal Coatings (DTMs) are specialized coatings designed for application directly onto metal surfaces without the need for a primer. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Waterborne and Solventborne coatings. Waterborne DTMs use water as a solvent, offering lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and easier clean-up, making them more environmentally friendly. Solventborne DTMs, on the other hand, use organic solvents, providing better adhesion and durability, but generally have higher VOC levels and require more extensive safety measures during application.</p></p>
