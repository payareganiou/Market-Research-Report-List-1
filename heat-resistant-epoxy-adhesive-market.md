<p><p>The heat-resistant epoxy adhesive market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component adhesives offer convenience and ease of use, as they are ready to apply right from the container and cure through heat exposure. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and hardener before application, allowing for enhanced customization of bonding properties. Both types are essential in industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics, where high-temperature resistance and strong adhesion are critical.</p></p>
