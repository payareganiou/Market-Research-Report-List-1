<p><strong>The Portable Fundus Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mydriatic Fundus Camera</li><li>Non-Mydriatic Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Fundus Cameras market is primarily divided into two types: mydriatic and non-mydriatic fundus cameras. Mydriatic fundus cameras require pupil dilation for optimal imaging, allowing for detailed examination of the retina, but often necessitate patient preparation. In contrast, non-mydriatic fundus cameras do not require pupil dilation, making them quicker and more convenient for both patients and healthcare providers, especially in mobile and primary care settings. Both types enhance accessibility to retinal imaging and facilitate early disease detection.</p></p>
