<p><p>Prenatal genetic testing encompasses various market types aimed at assessing the health of a fetus. Diagnostic tests like Chorionic Villus Sampling (CVS), Amniocentesis, and Cordocentesis provide definitive information about genetic disorders. Placental Biopsy and Fetal Biopsy analyze placental or fetal tissue, respectively. Screening tests, such as Carrier Screening, Sequential Screening, and Maternal Serum Quad Screening, assess the risk of genetic conditions without definitive results. These tests collectively aid in informed decision-making for expectant parents regarding potential health issues.</p></p>
