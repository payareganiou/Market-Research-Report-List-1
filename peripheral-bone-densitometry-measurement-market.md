<p><strong>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA)</li><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA)</li><li>Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA)</li><li>Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS)</li><li>Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry Measurement Market includes various technologies for assessing bone density. Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA) uses a single X-ray energy to measure bone mass. Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA) employs two X-ray energies for greater accuracy. Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA) uses standard X-ray images for bone density estimation. Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS leverages sound waves for non-invasive measurements, while Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT) provides detailed 3D imaging of bone structure and density.</p></p>
