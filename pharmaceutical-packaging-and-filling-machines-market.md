<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging and filling machines market is categorized into three main types: primary, secondary, and others. </p><p>Primary packaging machines directly handle the packaging of drugs, ensuring product integrity and sterility, often utilizing blisters or vials. Secondary packaging machines focus on grouping and protecting these primary packages, using cartons, cases, or shrink wraps for distribution and branding. The 'others' category encompasses specialized machines, including labelling, coding, and inspection equipment essential for compliance and quality assurance in pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
