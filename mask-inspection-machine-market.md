<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection Machine market is primarily categorized into two methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects masks by comparing die patterns directly, ensuring that defects are detected between successive layers. In contrast, the DB method utilizes a reference database of known good masks to identify discrepancies, enhancing quality control. Both methods are crucial for semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring high precision and reliability in mask production and ultimately improving yield rates.</p></p>
