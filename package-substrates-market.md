<p><strong>The Package Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCCSP</li><li>WBCSP</li><li>SiP</li><li>BOC</li><li>FCBGA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The package substrates market encompasses several types, including FCCSP (Fan-Out Chip Scale Package), which enables efficient thermal performance and miniaturization; WBCSP (Wafer-Level Ball Grid Array), providing compact connections; SiP (System in Package), integrating multiple functionalities within a single package; BOC (Ball-Out Chip), focusing on enhanced interconnectivity; and FCBGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array), allowing direct die attachment for improved performance. Each type plays a crucial role in miniaturizing electronic devices while optimizing performance and thermal management.</p></p>
