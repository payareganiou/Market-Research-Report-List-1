<p><p>Transorbs market types include Uni-Polar and Bi-Polar Transient Voltage Suppressors (TVS). Uni-Polar TVS devices are designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes in a single direction, making them suitable for positive voltage applications. In contrast, Bi-Polar TVS devices can clamp voltage surges in both directions, offering protection for circuits where the voltage can swing both positive and negative. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the electronic application being protected.</p></p>
