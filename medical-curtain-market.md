<p><p>The medical curtain market is categorized into two main types: reusable and disposable curtains. Reusable curtains are designed for multiple uses, made from durable materials that can endure frequent washing and disinfection, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly. In contrast, disposable curtains are single-use products, often made from lightweight, non-woven materials, ensuring high hygiene standards and reducing the risk of cross-contamination in medical settings. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining patient privacy and infection control in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
