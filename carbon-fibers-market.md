<p><p>The carbon fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: regular-tow and large-tow carbon fibers. Regular-tow carbon fibers typically have smaller fiber diameters and are widely used in applications requiring high strength-to-weight ratios, such as aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, large-tow carbon fibers feature larger fiber diameters, which reduce production costs and are favored in applications like wind energy and sporting goods. Both types serve distinct market needs based on performance characteristics and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
