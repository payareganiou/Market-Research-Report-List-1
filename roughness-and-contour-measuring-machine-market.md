<p><ul><li>Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li><li>Non-Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roughness and contour measuring machine market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact machines. Contact roughness and contour measuring machines utilize physical probes that touch the surface to gather data on texture and profile, providing precise measurements. In contrast, non-contact measuring machines employ optical or laser technologies to analyze surface features without physical contact, enabling faster measurements and reduced risk of surface damage. Both types cater to different industrial needs based on application precision and surface sensitivity.</p></p>
