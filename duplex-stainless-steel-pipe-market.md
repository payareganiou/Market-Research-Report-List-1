<p><p>The Duplex Stainless Steel Pipe market is categorized into four main types: Lean Duplex, Standard Duplex, Super Duplex, and Hyper Duplex. Lean Duplex offers improved corrosion resistance and lower nickel content, making it cost-effective. Standard Duplex provides balanced properties of strength and corrosion resistance, suitable for various applications. Super Duplex boasts higher chromium and molybdenum levels for superior strength and resistance to chloride environments. Hyper Duplex features enhanced strength and corrosion resistance, ideal for demanding industrial applications, especially in extreme conditions.</p></p>
