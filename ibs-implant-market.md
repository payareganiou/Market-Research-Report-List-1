<p><ul><li>Screwed IBS Implant</li><li>Non-screwed IBS Implant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IBS implant market is divided into two main types: screwed and non-screwed implants. Screwed IBS implants utilize a screw mechanism for secure fixation within the bone, offering enhanced stability and adaptability, making them ideal for complex cases. In contrast, non-screwed IBS implants rely on passive stability and friction to maintain their position, often allowing for easier placement and removal. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs, with choices influenced by specific patient conditions and treatment goals in implant dentistry.</p></p>
