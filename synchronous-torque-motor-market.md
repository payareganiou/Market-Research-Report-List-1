<p><p>The synchronous torque motor market is primarily divided into AC and DC types. AC synchronous motors utilize alternating current and are known for high efficiency and precision in speed control, making them ideal for industrial applications. Examples include permanent magnet synchronous motors. In contrast, DC synchronous motors operate on direct current, offering high torque at low speeds and simpler control mechanisms, which are useful in small applications and automotive systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
