<p><ul><li>Compressed Natural Gas Stations</li><li>Liquefied Natural Gas Stations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas refueling infrastructure market comprises two primary types: Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) stations and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) stations. CNG stations compress natural gas to high pressures for use in vehicles, offering efficient refueling for light-duty vehicles and fleet applications. In contrast, LNG stations liquefy natural gas for storage and transportation, catering primarily to heavy-duty vehicles and long-haul transportation, providing greater energy density. Both types play crucial roles in promoting cleaner vehicle technologies and reducing emissions.</p></p>
