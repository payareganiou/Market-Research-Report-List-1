<p><p>The endoscope reprocessing detergents market is divided into two main types: enzyme detergents and non-enzymatic detergents. Enzyme detergents contain specific enzymes that effectively break down organic materials, such as blood and tissue, enhancing cleaning efficiency. In contrast, non-enzymatic detergents rely on surfactants and other chemical agents to remove contaminants but may be less effective against stubborn residues. Both types are essential in ensuring the safe and effective cleaning of endoscopes, crucial for preventing infections in medical settings.</p></p>
