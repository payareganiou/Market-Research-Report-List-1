<p><p>The centrifuge equipment market is segmented into vertical and horizontal centrifuges, each serving distinct applications. Vertical centrifuges, designed with a vertical orientation, efficiently separate larger volumes of liquids from solids, making them ideal for wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, horizontal centrifuges feature a horizontal drum, optimized for continuous operation and high-capacity processing, commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and chemical production. Both types enhance separation efficiency and operational productivity, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
