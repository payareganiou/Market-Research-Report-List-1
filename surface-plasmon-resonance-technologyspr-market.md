<p><p>Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) technology is a powerful optical sensing technique that allows for real-time monitoring of molecular interactions. It exploits the resonance of surface plasmons—coherent electron oscillations at the interface between a metal and a dielectric material—when light is directed onto a metal surface. This phenomenon enables sensitive detection of biomolecular interactions, making SPR invaluable in fields such as biochemistry, pharmaceuticals, and diagnostics.</p><p>The SPR market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by increasing research and development activities in the life sciences, the demand for high-throughput screening methods, and advancements in sensor technology.
