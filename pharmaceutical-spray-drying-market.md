<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Spray Drying Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Nozzles</li><li>Two-Fluid Nozzles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical spray drying market involves various technologies, primarily categorized into pressure nozzles and two-fluid nozzles. Pressure nozzles utilize high pressure to force the liquid through a small orifice, creating fine droplets that rapidly dry into powder. In contrast, two-fluid nozzles mix the liquid feed with a compressed gas, typically air or nitrogen, resulting in atomization. Both methods aim to produce consistent, high-quality particles for pharmaceuticals, enhancing solubility and bioavailability while ensuring efficient manufacturing processes.</p></p>
