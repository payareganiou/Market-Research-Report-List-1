Gas turbine engines, prevalent in commercial and military jets, generate thrust through high-speed exhaust gases, providing superior power and performance. Other engines may include unconventional technologies such as electric or hybrid systems, gradually gaining traction for their potential to reduce emissions and operating costs.</p></p>
