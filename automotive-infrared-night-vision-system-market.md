<p><ul><li>Active Infrared Night Vision System</li><li>Passive Infrared Night Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System market is primarily divided into two types: Active Infrared Night Vision Systems and Passive Infrared Night Vision Systems. Active systems utilize infrared illuminators to emit infrared light, enhancing visibility in complete darkness by detecting the reflected light from objects. In contrast, passive systems rely on detecting natural infrared radiation emitted by warm objects, such as pedestrians and animals. Both systems aim to improve vehicle safety during nighttime or low-visibility conditions, enhancing driver awareness and prevention of accidents.</p></p>
