<p><p>Injectable hyaluronic acid face and lip fillers can be categorized into two market types: single-phase products and duplex products. Single-phase products consist of a uniform gel that provides smooth, even volume and is often used for subtle enhancements. In contrast, duplex products feature a dual-phase formulation that combines high and low molecular weight hyaluronic acid, allowing for tailored contours and longer-lasting results while maintaining a natural texture. Both types cater to diverse aesthetic needs in cosmetic procedures.</p></p>
