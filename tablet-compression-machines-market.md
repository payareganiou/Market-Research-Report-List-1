<p><ul><li>Single Station Type</li><li>Multiple Station Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet compression machines are classified into two main types: single station and multiple station. Single station machines compress powder into tablets at one station, ideal for small-scale production or lab settings, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, multiple station machines feature several compression stations, allowing for higher output and efficiency, suited for large-scale manufacturing. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring consistent tablet quality, but they serve different production capacities and operational needs.</p></p>
